More About Lynn

Lynn moved to Southern California to attend Pitxer College where she studied Theatre Performance and Psychology, and spent time abroad in Morocco. She also began a love affair with improv comedy.

After graduation, Lynn stayed in Los Angeles to continue her improv studies at The Groundlings then iO West and UCB, later performing all around Los Angeles and with Second City Theatricals on Norwegian Cruise Lines. She also pursued a career as an actor and writer, graduating from The Meisner Program at the Elizabeth Mestnik Acting Studio.

She recently relocated back to the Seattle and was thrilled to be accepted into Ada Developer’s Academy where she’s studying to become a software developer. Lynn spends most of her time catering to her spoiled but sweet rescue dog named Wesley. Watch out: he’s a licker.
