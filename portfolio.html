A sample of my learning

My Calculator project was one of my earliest works in Ruby and it was one that allowed us to demonstrate our understanding of user interaction and basic arithmetic in ruby.

One of our first pair programming projects was Rideshare. It required us to display and manipulate a large amount of data from a CSV file.

Rails zoo was a rails project that involved us creating an app that allowed users to add/delete animals from our "zoo".

The API muncher project was our first project utilizing an API, and one that I thoroughly enjoyed. Have a look and find something new and tasty to cook!

A smaller, and more recent project was called JS Digital Clock. We created this during our first week of JavaScript!

JS Scrabble was a redo of an older Scrabble project we originally wrote in Ruby.
