Ruby Projects

Solar System (2017)

Solar System is a Ruby project built to practice creating classes in Ruby. It's a terminal program that utilizes a Planet class and SolarSystem class. It asks the user what planet they would like to learn about and then outputs information about that planet. For instance, did you know that Saturn is a gas giant planet that is 72,367 miles in diameter? It's also 795.24 million miles away from Earth. How about that?

Websites

Flying Cat Creations (2013)

flyingcatcreations.com was built as a project for a web design class at Seattle Central Community College in 2013. It's a website for Seattle artist and small business owner Mutsuko Mitsui, who makes and sells ceramic pottery at the Pike Place Market. The site is designed responsively with layouts for computer screens, tablets, and mobile phones. Check out her shop to see examples of her beautiful work.
