My life in a few paragraphs

My first career was in veterinary nursing. I cared for patients in the ER/ICU for nearly 7 years. After, I pursued my Associates in Accounting and worked as a bookkeeper for several years.

It was after some encouragement from my partner that I began researching a career in software engineering. In the Fall of 2016, I was given the opportunity of a lifetime, and was admitted to the program at Ada.

In my spare time (which is nearly nonexistent these days!), I enjoy hiking, playing PC games, and painting. I consider myself a lifelong learner and thoroughly enjoy watching documentaries and researching new or unfamiliar subjects.
